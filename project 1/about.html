Web Development

We create and maintain websites.We are also responsible for the site's technical aspects, such as its performance and capacity, which are measures of a website's speed and how much traffic the site can handle. In addition, We may create content for the site.

Penetration Tester

As a penetration tester, we will perform authorised tests on computer systems in order to expose weaknesses in their security that could be exploited by criminals. We can choose to specialise in manipulating a particular type of system, such as: networks and infrastructures. Windows, Linux and Mac operating systems.
